Occupations in the US

This website provides information on employment by major categories for each state and state-like areas in the USA from 2016 to 2020.

​
​

Explore job counts by state with year

The below treemap shows the number of people employed in each of the 51 states in the US. Each box is zoomable to show the number of jobs broken down by major occupation categories. Click the header on top that starts with usa to zoom back out.
​

Explore Salary Trends by State and Year

This chart shows the average income for each of the major occupation category, broken down by year and state/territory/district. Use the dropdown to choose the year and state of interest.
​

Explore Salary Trends by State

The below US map shows the 51 US states, with the shade of color representing the total number of of people employed in each state. If you click on a particular state on the map, you can view the average income trends for all the major employment categories from 2016 to 2020.
